### What Worked
- Patience on Day 1 (Apr 28): held cash through extended SPX/SOX, avoided FOMO on semis +44% in 18 sessions.
- Sector-momentum framework picked the right leader: energy (+26% YTD, WTI breaking $100 on Iran/Hormuz) was the cleanest setup all week.
- 10% trailing stops auto-ratcheted up cleanly (XLE $53.20 -> $53.85, XOM $139.45 -> $140.12) without churn.
- Discipline on adds: held through XOM Q1 earnings + ISM PMI Friday rather than burning the last weekly slot for noise.
- Beat S&P 500 by +0.59% on a down week with only 40% deployed -- positive alpha despite low risk.

### What Didn't Work
- Underdeployed: 40% vs 75-85% target. Left ~$35-45k of capital idle and only captured a fraction of the energy move.
- Concentration: both positions are highly correlated energy names (XLE has ~24% XOM weight). One sector, one thesis, one headline = single point of failure.
- Friday gave back ~$495 (-0.49%) into XOM earnings + WTI fade -- a non-energy diversifier would have damped the drawdown.
- Did not scout/take the 3rd weekly trade in a non-energy sector (Industrials, Staples, Materials all co-leading) -- diversification slot wasted.

### Key Lessons
- "Patience > activity" cannot become "paralysis." If sector momentum is confirmed and entry rules are met, deploy -- waiting for a perfect pullback in a strong tape is just opportunity cost.
- Diversification is a hard rule, not a soft one. 40% of book in 2 names with 0.9+ correlation is a concentration bet, not a portfolio.
- Trailing stops did their job invisibly -- no manual intervention needed in week 1. Trust the system on stops.
- Earnings binary risk on individual names (XOM) is real but pre-announcement (8-K) materially reduced the surprise; held position correctly survived the print.

### Adjustments for Next Week
- Target deployment: push toward 60-65% by mid-week (not max 85% -- still feeling out the tape). Use the 3rd weekly slot Mon-Tue if a non-energy momentum setup confirms.
- Diversifier focus: scan Industrials, Staples, Materials for a clean entry to break energy concentration. PASS list still: semis, mega-cap tech, financials.
- Continue to NOT chase. Entry rules unchanged: specific catalyst, sector momentum, 7-10% stop, 2:1+ R:R.
- Watch CVX/COP/EOG Monday for energy thesis re-confirmation post-XOM print before adding any energy.
